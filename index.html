Welcome to the FTHS Tech Department!

Architecture

Learn about residential construction methods, design fundamentals, and various facets of the architectural and housing industries.

Autoshop

Students will learn about and get to work with real engines and automobiles.

Engineering Graphics

Students will learn about 3D modeling and design prototyping as well as being able to produce models using 3D printers, CNC routers, and laser cutters.

Graphic Design & Photography

Students take photos with high quality cameras and digitally create art using Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.

Video Editing

Students will film with high quality cameras, and edit videos using softwares like Adobe After Effects and Premiere Pro.

Honors Innovation and Design

This is the third year of any tech class, where students will hone their prior skills and work on projects that they are interested in.

IB Design Tech

This is the two year design course for IB students. It introduces them to the principles and intricacies of design in order to prepare them for the IB Internal Assessment.

Web Design

A program that freshmen get the chance to join later in the year. It begins their sophomore year, and continues for the rest of their time at FTHS. Gives the opportunity to take an NJIT class, as well as get certified through the NOCTI exam.

Woodshop

Students will learn the fundamentals of craftsmanship and the latest industry standards. They will familiarize themselves with hand tools, machinery, and design practices.
