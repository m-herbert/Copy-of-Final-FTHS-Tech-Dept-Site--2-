Meet The Teachers

Ms. Bott

Teacher

Ms. Bott has been a teacher at FTHS for 11 years. She has taught Digital Media and Photography 1 and 2, Video Communications 1 and 2, Honors Innovation and Design, Tech Design, and Interactive Design. She likes photography, working out, reading, and spending time with her dog. She majored in Photojournalism with a minor in Business at Ohio University, and she earned her Master's in Education focusing on Technology and Engineering at The College of New Jersey.

Mrs. Herbert

Teacher

Mrs. Herbert has been a teacher at FTHS for 6 years. She has taught Architecture I & II, Video Editing I & II, Interactive Design, IB Design Technology SL & HL, Honors Web Design, Honors Advanced Website Development, and Honors Innovation & Design. She loves traveling, going to the beach,and DIY projects. She is also the Technology Student Association (TSA) Co-Advisor. She studied at The College of New Jersey, earning a B.S. in Technology & Pre-Engineering Education.

Mr. Sutphen

Teacher

Mr. Sutphen has been a teacher for 31 years, and has taught at FTHS for 28 years. He has taught Intro to Woodworking, Cabinetmaking, Advanced Cabinetmaking, Carpentry, Tech Design Lab I/II, Engineering Graphics I/II, Architecture I/II, Auto I, and Graphic Arts I. He enjoys helping his students create their projects. He likes fishing, automotive repair and maintinence,cooking and hunting. He designed, drew the plans for, and constructed his own house. He attended Trenton State College, majoring in Industrial Arts Education.

Mr. Heubel

Teacher

Mr. Heubel has been a teacher at FTHS for 8 years. He has taught Auto I/II, Engineering Graphics, Honors Engineering Graphics, Video I/II, and Tech Design. In his free time, he likes to work on his truck and house and enjoys building engines and woodworking. He studied at California University of Pennsylvania, where he majored in Technology Education; attended Allegheny Community College, where he majored in Building Trades; and went to Local 12 Training Center, where he majored in HVAC and Fabrication.
