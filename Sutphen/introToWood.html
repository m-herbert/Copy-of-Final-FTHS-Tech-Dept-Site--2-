About The Class

Learn the Basics

Introduction to Woodworking is a basic exploratory woodworking course. Students learn craftsmanship through established industry standards including the latest techniques.

The students familiarize themselves with all available hand tools in addition to basic machinery and operations. The fundamental units of study are the properties of wood, construction methods, and finishing procedures.

All technical skills, woodworking techniques, consumer knowledge, environmentally sound practices, and safety regulations act as the foundational basis for post-secondary education and/or employment.
