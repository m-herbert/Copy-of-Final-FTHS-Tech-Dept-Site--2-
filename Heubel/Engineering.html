Engineering Graphics Classes

Engineering Graphics


In Engineering Graphics students learn how to rapidly prototype designs to understand the fundamentals of drafting. They will learn about the basics of 3D modeling, design prototyping, and problem solving skills to aid them in the future. Students will also be given the oppurtunity to produce models using 3D printers, CNC routers, and laser cutters.

Honors Engineering Graphics


In Honors Engineering Graphics students are given various problems and then are tasked with solving them by using their prior knowledge to develop a solution.
