IB Design Tech

An intro to the design process for IB Students

What is IB Design Tech?
For students that wish to pursue engineering and design in college, IB Design Tech is a great intro to these fields. Students can take higher level or standard level courses in IB that will prepare them for real world engineering and design issues. This course will also prepare students for the IB internal assessment which consists of solving a real world problem.

IB Design Tech 1
IB Design Tech 1 is focused on analysis, design development, synthesis, and evaluation. Students will learn design literacy by developing critical thinking and design skills, which can be applied in a variety of frameworks. Students will also utilize various CAD softwares to help hone in on the design and engineering process. The class is a great introduction to engineering and helps give students a basic understanding of what they will need to apply in IB Design Tech 2.
IB Design Tech 2
IB Design Tech 2 provides students with the means to analyze problems and develop and test solutions. Students will further develop their critical-thinking and design skills by applying them to practical contexts. Students will be tasked with hands-on projects and experiences to test their skills and prepare them for the IB Internal Assessment. Whether it be to solve a common problem that most of us face or coming up with a solution to a very niche issue, the test will gauge your skills and push you to be an ever better engineer.
IB Design Tech
Internal Assessment
The final test of this course. The IB Design Tech Internal Assessment. This will be accumulation of all of the skills students have learned thus far. Students will be designing and building to invent or innovate a product to solve a real-world problem.
