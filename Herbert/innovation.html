Honors Innovation and Design

A final course to hone and utilize your prior skills

The third year for any tech class, students from all over the tech department gather in this class to work on projects that will deepen their understanding of technology. Students must take a tech class for two years to take Honors Innovation. In this class, students will participate in dynamic, collaborative activities that will combine elements of each of their interests. This class will also teach students to bend to the constraints of a real world situation and find ways to be more efficient. The projects done in this class are all pertinent to the 21st century and can be utilized in a portfolio. There is a third honors innovation class for every tech class except for web design and IB Design Tech because they are separate programs.
For example, in Auto this course is much more independent than the previous two, truly giving students the opportunity to govern themselves. They will help with running the level 2 students restoration project as well as being given their own problems to solve using all of the knowledge they have gained from the previous two classes.
